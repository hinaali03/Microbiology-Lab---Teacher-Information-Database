Welcome to the Microbiology Lab

About

The Microbiology Lab of the Department of Biosciences is equipped with cutting-edge technology and provides hands-on learning for students and researchers. The lab focuses on various areas of microbiology, from microbial physiology to molecular genetics. Our faculty is dedicated to nurturing future scientists through comprehensive research and practical experience.

Faculty

The Microbiology Lab is guided by a diverse faculty with expertise in multiple fields. Our focus on both research and teaching provides students with hands-on learning experiences and supports graduate and undergraduate projects. Emphasizing interdisciplinary collaboration, we explore topics such as microbial resistance, biotechnology, and the role of microbes in human health, preparing students for leadership in the field. Our faculty is dedicated to nurturing future scientists through research and practical experience.
